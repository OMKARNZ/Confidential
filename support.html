Getting Started with Staking

Oxolot allows you to earn rewards on your digital assets through a process called Staking. By locking up your tokens in our validator nodes, you contribute to network security and earn APY.

How to Deposit

1. Navigate to your Wallet dashboard.
2. Select the asset you wish to deposit.
3. Copy your unique deposit address or scan the QR code.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA immediately after account creation. Go to Settings > Security to set up an authenticator app.
